Vastu For Flats

When you’re buying a flat in an apartment or looking to rent out a flat you should still look for certain Vastu aspects. A lot of people still think that Vastu is not important because their house is not touching the ground (i.e not on the ground floor) but Vastu doesn’t work that way. No matter how many floors up you are, you’re still connected to the ground. This is why we’ve listed 10 Vastu tips that will help you find an apartment that uses Vastu the right way.

Vastu helps to make your living comfortable in flats and prevent illness and other dangers from creeping into your life or relationships. The article brings some very important guidelines to be kept in mind while planning the architecture of a flat. Go through them and know the right Vasthu for flats, to make your life happy and prosperous.


  • The kitchen must be situated in the fire angle, i.e. in southeast. The cook should face east while cooking. The water tap in kitchen should be in the northeast direction.
  • The store room should be constructed in the southern part of the building. Grain and other provisions can be stored in the kitchen or in other rooms and cupboards. Things should not be stored in box beds, because it causes sleeping disorders.
  • The bathroom should be constructed in the west or south direction and its drains should flow towards northeas.
  • The drawing room should be in northwest, south or west direction.
  • The children's room should be constructed in the northwest corner. No bedroom should be located in the southwest direction.

North east bedroom for couples remedies

If a bedroom faces the northeast, implementing Vastu remedies and corrections can help balance the energy flow and create a more harmonious living environment. The key is to approach these remedies with a thoughtful and informed perspective, considering both traditional Vastu wisdom and modern practicality. While remedies provide guidance, consulting with a Vastu expert can offer personalized recommendations based on the specific characteristics of the bedroom and the unique needs of the occupants. Ultimately, creating a Vastu-compliant bedroom involves a combination of mindful adjustments, symbolic enhancements, and a holistic understanding of the energies at play.



Tips North east bedroom for couples remedies

  • One of the remedies for bedrooms in the north east is to keep a Vastu yantra in the north east direction. To correct Vastu doshas paint your bedroom colour as per Vastu in soothing shades of blue, yellow, or green.

  • If the bedroom is in the north east corner of the home, place the bed in the south west corner of the room. Sleep with your head towards the south. Sleeping with your head in the north is a big no, according to Vastu.

  • According to Vastu, sunlight has a profound effect on the positivity of the room. Allow the bedroom to be exposed to natural light for some time during the day. However, task-lighting prompts your brain to go into task mode. Opt for dimly lit lights to create a soothing ambience. If any bulbs in the bedroom go bad, replace them immediately.

  • According to Vastu Shastra, the guest bedroom should be in the Northeast. Regarding placing the bed, preference is given to the south or west part of the room. The south direction is the ideal direction to sleep, i.e. the head should be towards the south. Make sure there should be no beams above the bed.
