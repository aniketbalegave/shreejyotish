Vastu Defects/Faults

The science of Vastu Shastra is a colossal science requiring many years of deep study and intense research. It also requires a great deal of field experience prior to advising somebody on his either residential or commercial Vastu. One also needs to be adept on the art of balancing the norms laid down by the classical scriptures and modern constructional patterns. The arrangement of different areas in our home should be as per Vastu norms. Otherwise, it may create unrest in the occupant's minds, health problems and other problems in life. We are listing some of the vastu defects as below:

  • Furniture that is placed in the wrong direction.
  • Inappropriate colours, including that of the flooring.
  • Cooking direction.
  • Direction of toilet basins.
  • Incorrect direction of the puja room.
  • Toilet in North East.
  • Kitchen in North East.
  • Bedroom in North East.
  • Staircase in North East.
  • Cut in North East corner.
  • Overhead water tank in North East.
  • Septic tank in North East.

Vastu Shastra is a traditional Indian system of architecture that is used to design buildings, homes, and other structures. According to this system, the placement and design of buildings can have a significant impact on the lives of the people who live in them.

One of the key principles of Vastu Shastra is that there are certain defects or faults that can occur in a building's design or construction. These defects can have negative effects on the people living in the building, including health problems, financial difficulties, and relationship issues.

Some common Vastu defects include the placement of toilets or bathrooms in the wrong location, the presence of a staircase in the center of the house, and the use of dark colors in the interior design. These defects can be corrected through various Vastu remedies, such as the use of specific colors, the placement of certain objects, and the use of certain materials.

Overall, understanding and addressing Vastu defects is an important aspect of creating a harmonious and positive living environment. By following the principles of Vastu Shastra, individuals can create a space that promotes health, happiness, and prosperity.
