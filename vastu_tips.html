Vastu Tips

For a house to become a home, it needs to radiate the right kind of energy. According to a number of traditional beliefs, each home comes with its own energy type.

  • Ensure that there is good open space in and around you with the least number of obstructions. This will allow natural light and air to come in and keep your home fresh and cheerful always.

  • Grow plants in balconies or open spaces of the houses and not in the bedroom. This will help keep the air fresh and unpolluted. Also, the green color is very soothing for the eyes.

  • Place heavy furniture on in west or south-west direction of the living room. Area in the South-east direction of the living room can be utilized for electronics.

  • A south-east kitchen should always be preferred. This helps sunlight to come in the kitchen through its windows. Never have mirrors in kitchen, it generates negativity, ensure there is good hygiene maintained every time.

  • Prepare a checklist before buying the plot like legal issues, exact legal heirs of that plot. What points should be check when buying a plot.

  • Level of the land and roads around the plot.

  • Don't buy the plot which has Southwest water body.

  • Having elevated lands or Mountains towards east direction is said to be financial and health destructive plots.

  • Plots which have natural extensions towards the Northeast are very auspicious leading to all-round prosperity and health.

  • According to Vastu Shastra, the main entrance to a home is not only the entry point for the family, but also for energy.

  • In a home, the living room is where most of the activity is centred. It creates a favourable (or unfavourable) first impression when guests enter for social gatherings. Therefore, ensure the living room is clutter-free.

  • Brahmasthan is a unique feature of ancient Indian architecture based on Vastu Shastra. It is the centre of your abode and is considered to be the holiest and most powerful zone of the house.

  • Sleeping in a room with pungent smell adversely affects your peace and prosperity. To avoid this, leave the bedroom windows open for at least 20 minutes every day and allow fresh air to flow in.

  • Make sure that all clocks in your house are in working condition. It is said that slow or non-functional clocks symbolise delays or a stagnation in your finances.

  • One of the useful Vastu Shastra tips for wealth is to utilise the southwest corner of your home for placing the money safe.

  • Ensure that the drains outflow waste water properly without any kind of blockage. This Vastu tip for money suggests this may summon blocked finances.


Important Vastu Tips For New Home

Choose light and bright colours, such as white, yellow, and green, as these are associated with positive energy. Avoid dark and heavy colours, such as black and red, as these are associated with negativity. Plants are an important part of Vastu Shastra, as they bring positive energy into the vastu home.



  • The orientation of your home is critical in Vastu Shastra. Ideally, the main entrance should be facing east or north, as these directions are associated with positive energy. South and west-facing entrances are considered inauspicious.

  • The living room should be located in the northwest direction, as this is considered the zone of wealth and prosperity. Ensure that the seating arrangements are such that you and your guests face either north or east while seated in Vastu homes.

  • The kitchen should be located in the southeast direction, as this direction is associated with fire and energy. This will help to maintain a balance of elements in the Vastu home. The stove should be located in the southeast corner and the refrigerator in the northwest direction.

  • The master bedroom should be located in the southwest direction, and the bed should be placed in such a way that you can sleep with your head facing either east or west. Avoid sleeping with your head facing north, as this direction is associated with negativity.

  • Natural light is important in Vastu Shastra, so ensure that your vastu home is well-lit, with plenty of windows facing east or north. Avoid placing heavy furniture in front of windows, as this can block the flow of positive energy into the home.

  • The colours used in your home can also have an impact on energy flow. Choose light and bright colours, such as white, yellow, and green, as these are associated with positive energy. Avoid dark and heavy colours, such as black and red, as these are associated with negativity.


Vastu Tips for Money, Prosperity & Happiness




  • Keep the north-east corner of your home free of clutter and let it remain spacious for good energy glow.

  • According to Vastu Shastra, the money plant, rubber plant, bamboo plant and other similar plants are believed to attract wealth and prosperity.

  • Place a Kuber Yantra in Your Home. Lord Kuber symbolises affluence and prosperity and rules the northeast corner of your home.

  • The main entrance to your house is from where the Goddess Lakshmi will enter your house. So, keep it clean and decorate it.

  • Keeping plants at home is a great way to brighten up the house. The plants in purple color can bring financial profits in your home. If you can't find such plants, then a money plant is a well-known choice to bring wealth.

  • Fix Water Leakage Immediately Instances of water leakages from the bathroom, kitchen, or other areas should be immediately resolved.

  • No matter whether you want to bring in wealth, health or luck in your life, as per Vastu, you should always keep your house clean and organized.

  • Keeping medicines in a wallet or purse can bring bad luck. Hence, one should always avoid keeping the medicine in their wallet to avoid negative energy.

  • Keep your windows and mirrors clean and let the sunlight enter in your house. A dark room will only bring negativity in your life.

  • All your jewellery, money and important financial documents must be kept in the south-west (store such things in a cupboard or safe), facing north or north-east.

  • Placing small water objects within the house in the north east section can bring in good momentums through money and positive energy flow.
