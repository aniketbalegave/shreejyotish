Vastu For Career


Career is arguably one of the most important things in anybody’s life. Career is what all of us – eventually – try to build all the years while attending school, college and enrolling for higher studies. Highly successful people stay in a positive environment, so keep your space in the right tone. You will be more result oriented rather than being busy.

The Main door of your house also should be in a positive direction to have a career growth in a positive direction. Location of entrance towards the inauspicious direction may restrict your career growth. Do not seat below the beam while working at your desk at home or office. This may give you unnecessary pressure in work with no positive outcome.

If you are already in a job and looking for the promotion, you need to check your direction which you are facing on desk. Face east or north while working on your desk. It will help you in getting your hard work noticed by your seniors. Some people get promoted several times.

One of the key principles of Vastu Shastra for career success is the placement of the work desk. According to this science, the work desk should be placed in the south or west direction of the room. This is because these directions are associated with the planet Mars, which is known for its strong and dynamic energy. Placing your work desk in these directions can help you become more focused, productive and successful in your career.

Another important aspect of Vastu Shastra for career success is the use of colors. Different colors are associated with different energy patterns, and by using the right colors in your workplace, you can create a positive and harmonious energy flow. For example, shades of blue and green are known to promote calmness and concentration, while red and orange are associated with passion and creativity.
